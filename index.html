Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

Victory Sauce is a photography community based in Lisbon, run by Jaki Gerulskis. Our website is victorysauce.com.

What data we collect

We collect your email address when you sign up to The Drop, and your name, email, and message when you contact us via the contact form.

How we use it

Your email address is used to send you event notifications and occasional photography tips. We will never sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

We use MailerLite to manage our mailing list and Formspree to process contact form submissions. Both process data in accordance with GDPR. See their policies at mailerlite.com and formspree.io.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send. To request deletion of your data, email hello@victorysauce.com.
