Get serious about your photography, and have fun doing it. A full day in Lisbon with one of the most energetic portrait photographers working today.

We'll start the day with active learning, using Lisbon's most photogenic streets as backdrops. You'll spend time on both sides of the lens, taking turns swapping roles from photographer to subject, so that you can better understand how to put subjects at ease and direct portrait shoots with confidence. We'll learn to read outdoor settings quickly and troubleshoot on the fly. Wear something colourful.

After lunch, we'll reconvene at Absent to review the morning's shots and get inside Will's editing process. Learn his tricks to build that signature high-saturation look and find your own voice through colour and composition.

This workshop is limited to 12 participants, so everyone gets 1:1 attention from Will and real feedback on their work.

What you'll learn
Direct with confidence
How to put a subject at ease quickly and keep the energy high. Will's ability to make anyone comfortable in front of a lens is what separates his work — you'll learn how he does it.
Read a location
How to walk into an unfamiliar setting and immediately see what it offers: light, colour, geometry. Lisbon is one of the best locations in Europe for this.
Build a practice
We'll review the morning's shots and discuss how to hone your unique, recognisable voice. How do you select? What gets cut, and why? The goal is building discipline and judgement that turns a style into a practice.
Colour-first editing
A walkthrough of Will's post-production workflow: how he selects, sequences, and processes images for that high-saturation, joyful look that defines his work.
About the Photographer
Will Bremridge
Portrait · Travel · Lifestyle · LDN / LX

Based in both London and Lisbon, Will Bremridge is a portrait, travel and lifestyle photographer shooting for brands, ad agencies and magazines. With a heavy emphasis on bright colours across all his projects, Will aims to create work that is vibrant, joy-inducing and youthful. He heads up On-Set Portugal, producing California-like content for international brands in one of Europe's most reliably sunny cities.

Clients include AKQA · Virgin Atlantic · Fiat · British GQ · Esquire · Channel 4 · The Sunday Times Magazine · The New York Times Magazine · Sony · Billboard · Wired
